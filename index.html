Welcome to Quetta Sajji House!

At Quetta Sajji House, we bring you the authentic flavors of Quetta, Pakistan, crafted with passion and tradition. Our restaurant is dedicated to serving genuine, mouthwatering dishes that reflect the rich culinary heritage of the region, including our signature Sajji, flavorful Chargha, and aromatic Kabuli Pulao. Co-founded by Athar Ali and proudly sponsored by Mazhar Jewellers, Quetta Sajji House is more than just a restaurant – it’s a journey to the heart of Quetta’s vibrant food culture. We’re also excited to introduce our refreshing water flavors: Sparkling Lemon, Sparkling Water, and Natural Spring Water – the perfect complement to our delicious meals. Join us for an unforgettable dining experience where every bite and sip tells a story of tradition and taste.
